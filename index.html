Argentina

Argentina is a vast South American country with terrain that encompasses the Andes mountains, glacial lakes, and grasslands in the Pampas, the traditional grazing land of its famous cattle. The country is known for the dance and music of tango. Its sprawling cosmopolitan capital, Buenos Aires, is centered on the Plaza de Mayo, surrounded by imposing 19th-century buildings including the Casa Rosada, the iconic presidential palace..
